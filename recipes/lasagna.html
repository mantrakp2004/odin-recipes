Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a dish made of stacked layers of thin flat pasta alternating with fillings such as rag√π, cheese, vegetables, and seasonings. The dish is popular in many countries, and is commonly made with tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil. It is often baked in an oven.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 lb Italian sausage
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans canned tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 tablespoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef, sausage, onion, green pepper, and garlic over medium heat until no longer pink. Drain excess grease.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, sugar, oregano, basil, fennel seeds, salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, and cayenne pepper. Simmer, uncovered, for 1 hour.
  4. Spread 1 cup of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Layer with 4 uncooked las agna noodles, 1/2 of the ricotta cheese mixture, 1/2 of the mozzarella cheese, and 1/2 of the Parmesan cheese. Repeat layers.
  5. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes. Let stand for 15 minutes before serving.
